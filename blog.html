ICT field of work

In a constantly changing environment, companies and

public services investing in advanced digital technologies,

such as mobile communication, processing in traditional,

Scientific data analysis and smart devices are looking

for more and more people with ICT skills.







More emphasis has been placed on EU policies in recent years

on skills in information and communication technologies

According to Eurostat data, in 2015 about 8 million people

were employed as ICT specialists.

The share of jobs in the ICT sector in the total number of

employees increased in 2005-2015 by 35%.







The most sought-after ICT skills are:

  • 1. Internet security

  • 2. Business networks

  • 3. Big data analytics

  • 4. Mobile technologies

  • 5. Computing cloud

  • 6. Change management in the organization

  • 7. In-memory database

  • 8. Integrated product support

  • 9. Smart grid technologies or modern interfaces
